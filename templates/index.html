FAQ

Top Inquiries

What is network scanning, and why is it important?

Network scanning identifies devices and maps the structure of your network, helping you monitor and secure your infrastructure

How does your tool map network nodes?

Our tool automatically scans your network, detecting devices and creating a visual map of nodes for easy management

Can I customize alerts for network events?

Yes, you can set custom alerts to receive notifications about specific network events or changes

Is your network scanning tool secure?

Absolutely! Our tool includes built-in security features to ensure your network scanning process is safe and reliable

How do I get started with the network scanning tool?

Simply sign up for a free trial or contact us for a demo, and we'll guide you through the setup process

Does the tool support all types of devices?

Yes, our tool detects and maps a wide range of devices, including routers, switches, computers, and IoT devices
